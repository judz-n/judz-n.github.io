Hi there! My name is Judy and the Whimsy Dice Corner has been a long-time dream in the making - a passion project I have kept with me for almost 5 years before it became reality! I am a huge DnD fan and an even BIGGER fan of the magical fantasy aesthetic, so I have partnered with artists across Canada to provide TTRPG enthusiasts with a centralized haven to peruse all the uniquely desgined dice.

If you are a fan of supporting small businesses, supporting charity, AND buying pretty little rocks (ahem- I mean dice), then the Whimsy Dice Corner is the right place for you! Stay for as long as you like, feast your eyes on the sparkly, colourful dice, and you might find something to your liking. When you decide to buy something, an amount of the money gained will go to the Canadian Cancer Society, so it's a win-win all around and exactly what I envisioned for this little corner to be. ~^w^~
